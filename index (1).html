What's Inside

The science behind
the clarity

Every ingredient is selected for a specific role, dosed at levels consistent with published research. No proprietary blends. Full transparency.

GABA
Calm & Mental Balance

Gamma-aminobutyric acid is the brain's primary inhibitory neurotransmitter. It may help support a calmer mental state, reduce the mental noise that blocks focus, and create the conditions for clearer thinking.

Abdou et al., BioFactors, 2006
L-Tyrosine
Focus Under Pressure

An amino acid involved in the synthesis of dopamine and norepinephrine. Research suggests it may help maintain cognitive performance during demanding or stressful situations when neurotransmitter levels are depleted.

Deijen & Orlebeke, Brain Research Bulletin, 1994
Bacopa Monnieri
May Support Memory & Learning

An extensively studied adaptogen. Research suggests it may support memory consolidation and cognitive processing speed, particularly with consistent use over several weeks.

Pase et al., Journal of Psychopharmacology, 2012
L-Theanine
Calm, Alert Focus

An amino acid from green tea associated with increased alpha brainwave activity — a state of relaxed alertness. Research suggests it may support attention and reduce the edge of stress without sedation.

Gomez-Ramirez et al., Brain Topography, 2009
Alpha GPC
Acetylcholine & Memory Support

A highly bioavailable form of choline that supports acetylcholine synthesis — a neurotransmitter critical for memory, learning, and attention. Researched for its role in supporting cognitive performance.

Bellar et al., Journal of the International Society of Sports Nutrition, 2012
Phosphatidylserine
Cognitive Speed Support

A phospholipid that makes up a significant portion of brain cell membranes. Research supports its role in maintaining cell membrane health and has been associated with improved memory recall and processing speed.

Vakhapova et al., J. Alzheimer's Disease, 2010
Huperzine A
Acetylcholine Preservation

A natural compound that inhibits acetylcholinesterase — the enzyme that breaks down acetylcholine. By slowing this breakdown, it may help maintain higher levels of this key neurotransmitter for longer periods of focus.

Sun et al., Chinese Medical Journal, 1999
B-Vitamin Complex
Neural Energy & Neurotransmitter Support

B vitamins (including B6, B9, and B12) are essential cofactors in neurotransmitter synthesis and energy metabolism. They support the brain's baseline function and may help reduce mental fatigue over time.

Kennedy, Nutrients, 2016
